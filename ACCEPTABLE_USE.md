# Acceptable use policy / 可接受使用政策

You may use this project to export and analyze:

- your own Bilibili account data;
- data whose owner has knowingly authorized this specific analysis;
- synthetic or properly anonymized test data.

You may not use it to:

- harvest, profile, deanonymize, monitor, or publish data about people without appropriate authorization;
- obtain or reuse another person's credentials;
- operate bulk-account collection, surveillance, spam, credential stuffing, or data-broker services;
- bypass CAPTCHAs, platform safety controls, access restrictions, membership controls, or rate limits;
- perform automated likes, coins, follows, comments, posts, deletions, or other account mutations;
- overload Bilibili or conceal abusive request volume;
- falsely claim this project or its output is official Bilibili functionality.

Stop collection when the platform returns persistent anti-bot, authorization, or rate-limit errors. The maintainer may reject support requests that violate this policy.
