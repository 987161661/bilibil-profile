# Community code of conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, specific, and generous with context. Welcome beginners. Critique ideas and code without attacking people. Do not harass, threaten, discriminate against, impersonate, or expose private information about others.

## Privacy is part of conduct

Never post another person's account data, watch history, credentials, private messages, or inferred sensitive traits. Do not ask maintainers to recover accounts, bypass controls, or analyze a person without authorization.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content, close discussions, reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior threatens people, privacy, security, or the health of the project.

Report conduct concerns through a private channel described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) when public reporting could expose a person.

## Scope

This policy applies in project repositories, discussions, events, and public spaces where someone is representing the project.
